# The Design Rationale Document - UI/UX Designer

*This is a 2–3 page document in /docs/design-rationale.md. It explains your design decisions at the
system level. It must cover*:

1. Color palette and typography choices — and why they suit a knowledge management tool.
2. Navigation structure — how does the information architecture reflect how users think about
   knowledge?
3. The one design decision you are most proud of, and one you would change if you had more
   time.
4. Results of your usability walkthrough — what you found and what you changed.
